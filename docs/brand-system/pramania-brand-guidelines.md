# Pramania Brand Guidelines

## Approved direction
Concept 1 is the approved direction.

## Brand words
- clarity
- simplicity
- intelligence
- calm
- premium
- intimate

## Approved tagline
Career clarity, guided by intelligence

## Usage notes
- use the approved primary logo as the hero logo
- use warm parchment and soft ivory as main backgrounds
- use deep ink for primary text
- use champagne gold as the brand accent
- keep layouts spacious and quiet

## Do not
- add gradients
- use bright blue
- crop the tagline
- create glossy dark app icons
- thicken or distort the bloom mark

## Asset note
The logo assets in this pack preserve the approved concept visually.
The SVG logo files are visual SVG wrappers for quality preservation.
